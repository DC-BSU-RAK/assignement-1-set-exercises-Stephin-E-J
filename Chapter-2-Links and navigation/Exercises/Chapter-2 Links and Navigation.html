Overview

Interstellar(2014) is a science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan, it is set in a dystopian future where humanity is severely devastated by dust storms and crop failure, a group of astronauts tasked with finding a new habitable planet for mankind. The protagonist, Joseph Cooper(Matthew McConaughey) is a former NASA pilot and engineer, who works as a farmer and his children, Murph and Tom, with his father-in-law Donald.
Hidden from the public, NASA has discovered a wormhole appeared near Saturn, which leads to an another galaxy with twelve potentially habitable planets near the black hole named Gargantua. Cooper is enlisted to pilot the Endurance spacecraft through the wormhole as mission to colonize the habitable planet. Cooper is joined by Dr. Amelia Brand(Anne Hathaway), Doyle(Topher Grace), Romilly(David Gyasi) and a AI robot named TARS for their Journey.
As their journey began through space, Cooper is contend with the emotional weight for leaving his family behind, especially his daughter Murph, because cooper accepted against her wishes and promises to return. When she refuses to see him off, he leaves her his wristwatch to compare their relative time when he returns. After two-year of travel through the wormhole to Saturn. On the planets they visited, the crew faces an unexpected dangers, including time dilation caused by black hole's gravity, which distorts time for those in proximity.
